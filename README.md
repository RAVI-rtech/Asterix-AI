# Asterix AI (Project Codename)

> The Future of AI-Powered Learning

## Vision

Asterix AI is an intelligent Android application designed to become the world's most advanced AI learning assistant.

Unlike traditional AI chatbots, Asterix AI automatically understands the user's intent and chooses the best AI engine to complete each task.

The goal is to create a single AI companion capable of helping students learn, solve problems, code, research, summarize documents, understand images, and prepare for exams through one simple conversation.

---

## Mission

To make high-quality AI education accessible to every student through one intelligent assistant.
